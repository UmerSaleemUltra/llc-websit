FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to be a US citizen to work with BuzzFiling?

No, you do not need to be a U.S. citizen. As a non-U.S. resident, you can easily form a company with BuzzFiling without being physically present in the United States. We handle all aspects of setting up your U.S. company in any state you prefer.

How much does it cost to set up a U.S. company with BuzzFiling?

The cost of setting up a U.S. company with BuzzFiling consists of two main parts: the state fee (typically between $50 to $500, depending on the state) and BuzzFiling's service fee of $199, which includes company formation, obtaining an EIN, opening a bank account, and filing the required reports.

What information do you need from me to get started?

To start your U.S. company with BuzzFiling, we require your ID card (such as a National Identity Card or passport), your home address, and a suggested unique name for your company.

How long will it take to file my company?

The time to form your U.S. company varies by state. In states like Wyoming, we can complete the process in just one day, while others may take up to two weeks. After formation, we will obtain your Employer Identification Number (EIN) within 20 working days, and typically open your business bank account within a week thereafter.
